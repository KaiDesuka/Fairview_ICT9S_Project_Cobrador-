How to prevent fires
Exanples Explanation
Do not are at the very least, limit the use of Outlet Extenders. These extenders can overload the outlet it is connected to very easily and can cause fires and short circuits.
Keep flammable objects and materials that are used for fire specifically like leaves, fire wood, and etc away from the house or away from anything that can light them like matches.
Put out smoldering or smoking things properly, make sure that they have been fully put out because this can be a hazard.
Keep flammable liquids like cooking oil in containers and on/in shelves/cabinets to separate it from stoves as small accidents can cause big accidents.
